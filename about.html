How The Take Began

Suryagamezone was found as an inspiration, when a regular mom and dad of a 3 year old son saw the hardships of the two-year lockdown on their son. With outdoor play and socializing becoming difficult, the parents decided to build a place, where children could have a great time, play their hearts out and meet new kids to make friends. As the idea started taking shape, the playhouse also focused on helping kids develop their curiosity and widen their imagination, through different activities and workshops.

Today, Suryagamezone is a fun-zone that has a variety of games for the kids of all ages, and where parents can have a relaxing time, meeting other parents, while knowing that their kids are in safe hands, without having to worry about safety, cleanliness and hygiene.

A Few Basic Guidelines

TIMINGS

We are open 7 days a week, from 11.00 AM to 8.00 PM. One entry ticket will be valid for 1 hour.

DRESS CODE

Shoes are not allowed in the play area, so socks would be compulsory at all times.

ADVANCED BOOKING

Although we are open for walk in, we recommend that you book your convenient slot beforehand, to avoid overcrowding, and to check availability.

HEALTH & SAFETY MEASURES

Children who might be feeling unwell, or who are ill will not be allowed within the play area.

The happiness of kids is always our first priority. Bring your kids and give them chance to play and learn only at the Suryagamezone.
